Destinations

A quick, practical selection—good for a first visit.

Trekking Regions

  • Annapurna Circuit — classic loop with lodges, varied scenery, Thorong La pass (5416m). Best: Oct–Nov, Mar–Apr.
  • Everest Base Camp — iconic route; allow acclimatization days. Lukla flights can be weather‑dependent.
  • Langtang Valley — reachable by road from Kathmandu; glacier views and Tamang culture.

Cities & Sites

Kathmandu Valley

World Heritage squares, stupas and artisan alleys. Stay in Thamel or Patan for easy access.

Pokhara

Lakeside town with paragliding, boating and Annapurna views. Gateway to many treks.

Chitwan

Lowland jungle for rhino and bird watching. Choose ethical safari operators and avoid elephant rides.
