Culture

Nepal is a mosaic of languages, faiths and festivals. Be curious and respectful.

Etiquette

  • Remove shoes before entering homes and many temples.
  • Use your right hand when giving/receiving items.
  • Dress modestly at religious sites; ask before taking photos.

Food to Try

Daal Bhaat

Lentil soup, rice and sides—nutritious and common across the country.

Momo

Steamed dumplings with achar (sauce). Popular everywhere—veg or meat.

Newari Dishes

From yomari to bara—try in the Kathmandu Valley.
