Travel Tips

Keep it simple and safe. These basics go a long way.

When to Go

  • Oct–Nov: clear skies for trekking; busiest season.
  • Mar–Apr: warmer, rhododendrons bloom.
  • Dec–Feb: cold at altitude; great for city culture.
  • Jun–Sep: monsoon; lush but wet, leeches on trails.

Health & Safety

  • Altitude: ascend slowly; “climb high, sleep low”.
  • Water: use bottled or purified. Carry a filter if trekking.
  • Insurance: make sure it covers trekking above 3000m if needed.

Money & Connectivity

  • Currency: Nepalese Rupee (NPR). ATMs in cities; carry cash in rural areas.
  • SIM: local prepaid SIM cards available with passport (NTC, Ncell).
  • Power: Type C/D/M sockets; frequent outages in remote areas—carry a power bank.
