Visit Nepal 2026

See more. Travel kindly. Celebrate the Himalayas, heritage, and the heart of Nepal.

Our Motto

  • See More — discover every corner of Nepal, from Everest to Lumbini, from lakes to temples.
  • Travel Kindly — respect the culture, protect nature, and leave only footprints behind.
  • Celebrate Together — share smiles, stories, and the spirit of Nepal with the world.

Why 2026?

2026 marks a renewed spirit for tourism in Nepal — cleaner trails, stronger local communities, and a fresh invitation for the world to explore. From adventure seekers to peace lovers, Nepal welcomes everyone to experience its beauty and kindness.

Our Vision

  • Promote sustainable and responsible tourism.
  • Empower local guides, homestays, and family businesses.
  • Showcase Nepal’s true identity through culture and nature.

Message to Visitors

Come for the mountains, stay for the people. Every sunrise over the Himalayas reminds us that Nepal’s beauty lies not only in its landscapes but also in its warmth and hospitality.
